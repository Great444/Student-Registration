Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

Why can't I access my courses?

Ensure your account is activated and you're using the correct login credentials. If issues persist, contact support.

Where do I find my grades?

After logging in, navigate to the "Academic Records" section in your dashboard.
